Meet Your Doctor

Dr. Yashshree
Nagargoje

Dr. Yashshree Nagargoje is a dedicated dental professional with a strong commitment to community health and patient well-being. A BDS and MPH graduate, she has spent years bridging the gap between clinical excellence and public health.

During the COVID-19 pandemic, she served as a frontline Covid Warrior with the BMC, managing multiple CCC-2 centres and earning accolades for her extraordinary dedication during India's toughest healthcare challenge.

She then served as Head Dentist at Adani Hospital, Adani Township — providing care to 15,000+ residents and conducting 12+ dental camps with 100+ attendees each.

  • Recognised by BMC for outstanding COVID-19 contributions
  • Managed 4+ CCC-2 centres across Mumbai during the pandemic
  • Led dental outreach for 15,000+ residents at Adani Township
  • Conducted 12+ dental health camps with 100+ attendees each
